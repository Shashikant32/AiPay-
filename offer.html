"Refer and Earn" offer for Recharge

A "Refer and Earn" offer for recharge typically allows users to earn rewards or discounts by referring friends to use a particular service.

Step 1: Referral Code

You receive a unique referral code when you sign up for a service, such as a mobile recharge platform or app.

Step 2: Sharing the Code

You share this referral code with your friends, family, or contacts through messages, social media, or email.

Step 3: Referral Use

When someone uses your referral code to sign up and makes their first recharge, both you and the person you referred are eligible for rewards.

Step 4: Earning Rewards

You earn cashback, recharge credits, or discounts on future recharges. The referred person may also get a sign-up bonus, such as a discount on their first recharge.
