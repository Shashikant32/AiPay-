Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

These questions cover a variety of topics including how the program works, rewards, and account management.

What is the referral and earn program?

The referral and earn program allows users to refer friends, family, or others to sign up or purchase on our website. In return, you earn rewards, credits, or commissions for each successful referral.

How does the referral and earn program work?

To get started, simply sign up for an account on our website. Once you're logged in, you can access your unique referral link or code. Share this with others, and when they use it to sign up or make a purchase, you'll earn rewards.

How can I refer someone?

You can refer someone by sharing your unique referral link via email, social media, or messaging apps. Your friends or family can then use this link to sign up or make a purchase.

What do I earn for referring someone?

You earn rewards such as credits, discounts, or cash commissions for every successful referral. The exact amount or type of reward depends on the referral program terms.

How do I know if my referral was successful?

Once your referred friend or customer has signed up or made a qualifying purchase, you'll receive a notification or email confirming the success of your referral, and your reward will be credited to your account.
